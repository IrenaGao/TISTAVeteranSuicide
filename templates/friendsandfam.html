For veterans:

  1. Chat online with trained counselors at www.VeteransCrisisLine.net
  2. Use the smartphone app called Virtual Hope Box to view photos of friends and family, videos of loved ones, and music - tailored specifically to your needs!
  3. Avoid drugs and alcohol - they can cause you to make poor decisions.
  4. Remember your value that your loved ones have for you!

For friends and family:

  1. Be available and offer support. Ask questions such as:

    • How are you feeling?
    • Do you ever feel like just giving up?
  2. Be respectful and acknowledge the person's feelings, offering reassurance that things can get better.
  3. Encourage the person to seek treatment from a doctor or mental health provider.
  4. Remove potentially dangerous items from the person's home, if possible.
